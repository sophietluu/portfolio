Overview

Build a shelf that can be easily assembled without the use of any adhesives, nails, screws, clips, or other fasteners — with a minimum holding weight of 200 lbs. This simple bookshelf accomplishes all that and more with an easy-to-follow instruction manual.

The final design holds over 300 lbs, enough for both you and your books. All pieces slot together using precisely cut joints, making the shelf fully tool-free to assemble and disassemble.
